CONTROLLER & CONTROLLEE

“CONTROLLER & CONTROLLEE” is a wearable  device that one person can “control” the person who wear it  by using joystick. The people who are controlled by other people is called “ Controllee”. The controllee can get vibration pulse from belt worn by controllee and it will let controllee know which directions to go. Controller should control the controllee by using the joystick  to reach different dots following the order from  puzzle cards. This cyborg experiment can explore the relationship between two people. How does controllee  understand the vibration pulses. What is the controller’s feeling of controlling other people to reach the goal.The “Controller and Controllee” project is one of the results of my MFA class-design studio: cyborg, in which we’ve focused on the relationship between human to human, human to machine, machine to machine.

PROJECT INFO

Fall 2017 | Individual Project
“Controller and Controllee” is a four-week project. I got a start point-“telepresence”-to come up with and dream a new bold speculative way of disembodying, like what face-time did. This cyborg can replace us to attend at any long-distance space.

So, I am wondering that in the future world, our body is hackable. DESIGN PROCESS

Inspired by the leather harness, I came up with the idea that making a wrist belt with eight vibration motors and connect with a joystick. The person(Controllee) who wear the wristbelt can be manipulated by the person (Controller) who own the joystick.

RULE

I designed the rules for the experiment. User should follow the instructions showed on the selected puzzle card to test the posobility of hackable body.

INSTRUCTION: PUZZLE

STEP 1: 
Find a partner, one person play the controller, one play the controllee. 
STEP 2:
Controller should give the controllee a sense of direction by using a hand-hold joystick following the instruction showed on puzzle cards.

Controllee should wear an eye-mask and the belt and follow the vibration hint from belt. Following the the sense of vibration and reaching the goals. 
TIPS: TRUST YOUR PARTNER
