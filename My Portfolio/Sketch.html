QUICK CYBORG

Fall 2017 | Individual Project
QUICK CYBORG is a serise of exploration around three topic, " Extended Vision" "Prosthetic" "Disembody".

Quick Cyborg #1: Extended Vision

1 Week
For this project, you will create an apparatus that in some way expands or enhances your vision. The apparatus should directly engage the human body in some way. It should build on and enhance human abilities of vision.

SEPARATED EYES

Separated Eye is a device that consist of a wearable helmet, a wrist belt attached to a camera, a channel attached to a screen in which you can see different view from it. From the other side of channel, instead of the original view, the front view from eye. you can get other different views of the environment separately by switch the camera attached to the wrist belt.

TESTING

When I was making this device, I decided the length of the channel in terms of whether it is suitable for human eyes. I found a interesting phenomenon. When I wear this device on my left eye. My brain can not process the information/image from left eye, which is different from the information/Image from normal right eye. And I feel dizziness when have different views. Only when our two eyeballs accept same information, we can understand the meaning of image without dizziness. However when I wear the device in right eyes, the dizziness situation had been relived. My right eye are more adaptable to process different information than the left eye. Some people’s left eye are more adaptable than the right. I am not sure the reason why it have so many difference when our eyes accept different views separately. But it is a interesting phenomenon discovered in this testing.

Quick Cyborg #2: Extra Limb

1 week
The goal of this project is to design and model a physical prototype of a prosthetic device that attaches to the human body. The object should explain how your invention gives the user new capabilities, while also considering the particular context of use.

Energetic Joint

The device showed in pictures is Energetic Join which is a attachable device can translate your kinetic energy into electricity when you walk or run. The idea drives from the scene that a man hold a smart phone which connected with a charge cable from his bag, and walked in a street. It looks like he can generate electricity by herself. The principle of it is very simple and it is similar to the principle of Hand Crank generator which can supply 10 watt 120 Volts and Hand Flashlight.
