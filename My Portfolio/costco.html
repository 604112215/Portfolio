COSTCO SHARING

Costco Sharing is a new service designed for the people who are single but concern about buying a huge amount of items at Costco because they are single. They can not afford or finishes such amount of products on time. Costco Sharing solves this problem, and it will attract tons of new customers to be a Costco Member. This service is both good for customer and Costco itself.

APPROACH

In our team, We conducted various research methods to get the final insights. For example, we did Service Safari to experience how people are shopping at Costco and find some problem. We used the Contextual Inquiry to see users’ shortcut of making a decision.

MY ROLE

Everyone on our team played important roles. We divide the tasks to maximize our work progress. I’ve worked on collecting data on the research process, building physical environment prototype including shopping cart, shelf, locker, etc. By using foam core, designing a screen clipper by using 3D printers and leading an origami practice to come up with initial insights.

DESIGN PROCESS

SERVICE SAFARI

We went to Costco and experienced how to shopping at Costco and what kind of services that Costco provide to users.

BUSINESS ORIGAMI (Part A): PROBLEMS

To find the problems and pain points that users are facing, we did a business origami after finishing the service safari. We played different roles with different concerns and situations and wanted to know what kind of decisions they made and experiences they are experiencing.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

After doing Business Origami, we find a problem that young people who are single are easy to decide between giving up buying some stuff when they are shopping at Costco? Thus we made a contextual inquiry about what kind of factors will influence the decision making of buying things at Costco.
After doing research, we got some factors that influence this decision making.

KEY FACTORS OF MAKING DECISION AT COSTCO

We came up with five key factors that influence users' decision making when they are shopping at Costco. People may give up buying something when they concern those factors.

BUSINESS ORIGAMI ( Part B ) - SOLUTION: SHARING SERVICE

After defining the problems that user are facing, we did the business origami again to figure out a possible solution to solve this problem. In this part, we come up an extra service-Share Service-in to users’ shopping journey. If the volume of a good is too big, users can share the rest of the product with other users. Even this solution is a bold, rough one it revealed users’ mental model of sharing a product at Costco and shortcut of facing this problem. Indeed, in the processes of doing the business origami to perform different situations, we did find many side-effects caused by this new service.

PROBLEMS

Calculating sharing activity is always a hard thing. There are a lot of potential problems if we want to make this service come true.

1. If people only buy the amount of what they need, It will damage the business model of Costco. The business model of Costco is to sell a significant amount of product. In this way, Costco can earn the substantial profit from it. However, If it starts to offering sharing service, that means Costco may not sell out the same volume as the past and customer will only buy the portions of the product based on their needs.

2. If this service becomes true, Costco will face the storage problem and pipeline problem. There are some situations.

INSIGHT​S

I draw some initial ideas when doing a interivew

Upgrade Membership Card

Customers can upgrade membership card for Sharing service.
Pros:
It‘ll help divide the customer who needs sharing service from another customer.
It can relieve waiting line problems and storage problems caused by this sharing service.
It’ll give Costco extra money

Supporting Screen

The screen can be attached on Costco shopping cart.
It will show you sharing items around you based on your nearby shelves when you are shopping.
Customer will add sharing item in the screen.

Fixed Portion

Customer Only can choose the fixed portion of sharing items.
In this case, It can solve storage, back-side stage problems and make this service success.

KEY LEARNING

1. Think question chronologically. Service Design is a huge construction. When doing service design, We should think questions in a different position. How individuals interact with the organization. How every single interaction related to other parts of the organization.

2. Think about side-effect. Each solution when we come up with will cause some side-effect, not just focus on a solution but also focus on how to side-effects created by solution and try to come up with a better assumption.
