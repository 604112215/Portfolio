MIRROR

Do you really know how to take care of your skin?

Mirror is a mobile application using the computer vision to analyze users' skin conditions and help people who want to change their bad behavior of taking care of skin and then build good behaviors with healthy skin instructions. r. It will help them have a better skin condition.

01. Scan your skin

Mirror uses computer vision to grade your skin and gives you a right skin diagnosis. The diagnosis will show you what is going wrong on your skin and what kind of skin care fits you.

02. Upgrade your skin score

Doing the skin maintenance listed on TO-DO can get extra stars after finished it. Those starts can upgrade your skin score and help you get a better rank among your friends.

03. Buy skin care based on your diagnosis

In the diagnosis record, Mirror will tell what kind of skin problems you have and recommend the skin care products suitable for your skin. These will also be shown on the TO-DO list.

What changed on Mirror 3.0

I used Framer.js to make motion design by using CoffeeScript and adding feedbacks to respond to each interaction.

Mainpage
1. Based on testing, I found that users cannot get any useful information from the abstract skin problems table on Mirror 2.0. Thus I changed this table for a Skin Diagnosis. The skin diagnosis will be created after grading skin.
2. By pressing the Grade Button under the mirror, users can grade their skin condition.
3. A Scan animation (the big circle) has been added to the mirror, and it helps users know the camera is working.

To-Do
1. Users can directly know how many scores they get after finishing one maintenance through the Score Review above the TO-DO list.

Product details page
1.If the user light on the stars, it means it will be unlocked on the To-Do list and users can do this new unlocked skin maintence to get score reward.

Skin score record - Rank
1. I deleted Skin Score Record page and replaced it by Ranking page because the historical record is so ambiguous for a user to track his history states and pointless. However, the ranking page can show the user her/her rank among her/his friend, which can improve her/his motivation.
