MIRROR

Do you really know how to taking care of your skin?

Mirror is a mobile application that use computer vision to analyze users' skin conditions and help people who want to have healthy skin instructions to change their bad behavior of taking care of skin and the build a good behavior. It will give them a better skin condition.

Please try Prototype: https://framer.cloud/KdoLU

01. Scan your skin

Mirror use computer vision to grade your skin and give you a right skin diagnosis. The diagnosis will show you what is going wrong on your skin and what kind of skin care fits you

02. Upgrade your skin score

Doing the skin maintenance listed on TO-DO can get extra stars after finished it. Those start can upgrade your skin score and help you get a better rank among your friends.

03. Buy skin care based on your diagnosis

In the diagnosis record, Mirror will tell what kind of skin problems you have and recommend you skin care suit for your skin. It also will show on TO-DO list.

What changed on Mirror 3.0

I used Framer.js to make motion design by using CoffeeScript and add feedbacks to respond to each interactions.

Mainpage
1. Based on testing, I found that users can not get any useful information from the abstract skin problems table on Mirror 2.0. Thus I changed this table to Skin Diagnosis. The skin diagnosis will be created after grading skin.
2. Adding a Grade Button under the mirror. Users should press it to grade their skin.
3. Adding Scan animation in the mirror(the big circle), it helps users understand the camera is working.

To-Do
1. Adding Score Review above todo list, User can directly know how many scores they get after finishing one maintain.

Product details page
1.If the user light on the start, it means it will be unlocked on the to-do list. Users can do this new unlocked skin maintain to get score reward.

Skin score record - Rank
1. I deleted Skin score record page and replaced it by Ranking page because the historical record is so ambiguous for a user to track his history states and pointless. However, the ranking page can show the user the rank among her/his friends. It can improve users’ motivation.
