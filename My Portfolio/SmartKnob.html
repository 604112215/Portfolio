SMART KNOB

Have you ever been annoyed by over-cooking food and failure handing multi-task when cooking food?

Smart Knob is an IoT product that can transfer your old stove to smart one by replacing the old knob. The Smart Knob can be embedded in a voice control system like Amazon Echo, Google home. Thus It can be your cooking assistant to help you to master heat by just communicate with echo.

Mobile Walkthrough

When I was designing the Smart Knob (IOT product). I realized that it is very essential for the user is to recognize the different states of the digital product in reality. Using After Effect, I designed different animations for each state to let user understand different states of Smart Knob.

1.Transfer Your old knob to digital one

You can transfer the old snob to a smart knob just by replacing it. The smart knob will be waked up by closing your phone with supporting app to it. Then you can connect the smart knob into a wifi network. and then share data with voice control systems like Amazon Echo, Google home etc.

2.Wake up it by close to it

When you open the supporting app for the Smart Knob, you will close to it and connect it into your wifi network.

3.Free your hands-Smart Knob + Amazon Echo/ Google Home

Smart Knob can free your hands because you can give it command of changing heat temperature by communicating with it through voice communication systems like Amazon Echo or Google Home.

4.Project Recipe to Smart Knob

You can download a recipe from supporting app and project to the smart knob. The Knob will change the heat temperature step by step based on the recipe. In the design process, I defined THREE different phrases of projecting recipe to the device.

·Ready

it is to show a user that the Smart Knob has done the downloading.

·Blink

Blink is halfway status between Ready and Cooking. It shows a user that device is always stand by and waiting to trigger if users are ready to start.

·Cooking

When you are ready, you tell the device to start. It will show you which step you are in and how high the temperature is.

·Ready

·Blink

·Cooking

5. Twist it to stop( offline status)

You can stop the cooking programming by simple twist it. I keep the physical interaction of twisting to adjust the temperature when the connection of internet doesn't work well (offline).

DESIGN PROCESS

INTERVIEW

BEGINNING

As the second project of the Prototyping class. This project is to design an IoT related project. We were challenging to find any topic that interested ourselves. IoT related project is becoming harder and harder as time goes because many ideas have been done by many companies an innovators.

To find something interested, I turn to interviewed some of my friends and asked: “ What device do you think are still stupid in your home?”

After doing it, I found a problem: Our stoves are not smart enough for modern people.

INITIAL IDEAS

I draw some initial ideas when doing a interivew.

VALUE PROPOSITION STATEMENT

PROBLEMS SCENARIO

To get more details of situations that may face when cooking. I made a Problems Scenarios and described some problems and pain points people might face.

PAIN POINT

1. Unit Conversion
The temperature showed in recipes cannot map to the scales on the stove.

2.Rental Apartment
A lot of young people are renting a room or an apartment in a big city. It is not possible for people to change the whole stove to a smart one in a rental apartment.

3.Chaos created by limited hands
When people are cooking, they used to handle a lot of tasks at the same times, prepare the rest of ingredient, adjust heat temperature, take care of cooking time and the heat on a pan. It is not easy for people to handle such tons of tasks at the same time just with two hands. Chaos created from this.

INSIGHT​S

1.Conversational Interface​​

Users can set heat temperature by talk to the Smart Knob. They can set a temperature for a few minutes.

2.Coordinate heat change with recipes

Users can cook food by following the recipe showed on supporting app for Smart Knob. The heat temperature will be changed based on the steps you are on.

SITE MAP

Network of IoT

The network that behind the product is crucial to UX design. The system of IoT I choose will influence how the User Experience could be. Instead of using the gateway as a bridge to connect a device with internet. I decide using WIFI Router as a bridge to connect Smart Knob to the WIFI network. Here are two significant advantages of it:

1. It can use the conversational power of Google Home or Amazon Echo by sharing data and analyzing data.

2. The people who rent a room are always moving. Connecting the Internet make the device more portable.

KEY LEARNING

1. Using Foam core to build low-fidelity scenario can help bring audiences to your scenarios. It is an easy way to let users feel empathy.

2. The system of IoT is more complicated than a single application implemented on a smartphone because the IoT system should coordinate with reality, actuator, sensor, etc. It will face many challenges like connecting wifi will use much power, the power challenge for IoT devices.

3. For IoT product, Network of it will influence how to design UI and UX.

4. Off-line states is an essential part for IoT product because IoT devices often connect intermittently. It has a high risk of latency and networks are not 100% reliable. By contrast, people expect physical things to respond to us immediately. Thus the exit plan is essential to the IoT device.

WHAT WENT WELL

1. When testing, people were excited about the product.

2. The way of making prototype and test goes well. It brings the magic moment to users.
