SMART KNOB

Have you ever been annoyed by over-cooking food and struggling multi-task by hands when cooking food?

Smart Knob is an IoT product that can transfer your old stove to the smart one by taking the place of the old knob. The Smart Knob is set to embedded in a voice control system like Amazon Echo, Google home. Hence, there is no doubt that it is conducive to your cooking such as when taking control of heat by communicating with Echo.

Mobile Walkthrough

When it came to the design of the Smart Knob( IoT product), a seemingly creative thought just cropped up in my mind. Clarity of device and experience is crucial to the user to get better satisfaction. With After Effect, I designed different animations for each different state of the product to make users better access and understand the product. Subsequently, the apprehension of the whole process deepened.

1.Transfer Your old knob to the digital one

It is easy for the user to replace the old knob to the Smart Knob.

2.Wake up it by close to it

Once your phone with supporting app is closing to Smart Knob, It comes into effect. Later on, a wifi network is ready to be connected with it. In a not only convenience but also simplicity way.

3.Free your hands-Smart Knob + Amazon Echo/ Google Home

One of the advantages about Smart Knob is that it can relieve your burden of hands using accepting the command. For instance, it can assist you in tuning heat temperature according to voice communication systems like Amazon Echo or Google Home, etc.

4.Project Recipe to Smart Knob

It is also an excellent choice to download a recipe from supporting app and project it to the smart know. Due to the recipe, the whole process such as tuning heat temperature seems to be easy to understand in details. By following the recipe, users can reach the expected outcome according to specific preferences. In design, three different phrases of projecting recipe defined.

·Ready

The whole process of downloading can be viewed clearly for users.

·Blink

Blink can be considered as a transition between Ready and Cooking. It can make it more convenient for users to keep eyes on whether device is on or not.

·Cooking

Upon the device starting, it will shed light on the specific step as well as temperature for users.

·Ready

·Blink

·Cooking

5. Twist it to stop( offline status)

You can stop the cooking programming by simple twisting the Smart Knob Whenever the internet connection fails to operate well or not. Thus it is advisable to maintain the interaction of twisting.

INTERVIEW

BEGINNING

As the second project of the Prototyping class. This project aimed at designing an IoT related project. It is a struggle for us to seek any topics which can draw our attention, As it stands, this project has boosted numerous potentials, but it can be challenging. In fact, since a wide array of innovative designers has obtained plenty of accomplishments in this field, there is little chance to continue to carry on IOT related project.

In the pursuit of something appealing, I determined to interview several friends and asked: “ What device do you think are still stupid in your home?”

After doing so, a lingering problem has sprung up: Currently, the stove is far from smart so than a new kind which can cater to user’s needs.

INITIAL IDEAS

I came up with some initial ideas when doing a interview.

VALUE PROPOSITION STATEMENT

PROBLEMS SCENARIO

To delve deeply into the practical situation when cooking, I put forward a Problems Scenarios and posed some problems related to pain points people might encounter.

PAIN POINT

1. Unit Conversion
The temperature showed in recipes cannot apply to the function of the stove.

2.Rental Apartment
An increasing number of young people choose to rent rooms, especially in a metropolis. Therefore, there is no denying that changing a stove with smart function is beyond the reality. In other words, there still exists a stove in the majority of houses.

3.Chaos created by limited hands
During the cooking time, people used to handle a lot of tasks at the same time, such as preparing the rest of ingredients, adjusting heat temperature, keeping eyes on cooking time and the heat on a pan. To be honest, completing all tasks at the same time requires a diversity of efforts. That is how chaos created from this.

INSIGHT​S

1.Conversational Interface​​

Users can set heat temperature by talk to the Smart Knob. They can set a temperature for a few minutes.

2.Coordinate heat change with recipes

By following the suggestions from a recipe, users can be better at cooking food. The heating temperature also will be changed on the step you choose.

SITE MAP

Network of IoT

The network that behind the product is crucial to UX design. The system of IOT I choose will exert significant influence on how the User Experience could be. Instead of using the gateway as a bridge to connect a device with internet. I tend to use WIFI Router as a bridge to connect Smart Knob to the WIFI network. Here are two significant advantages of it:

1. when it turns to share data and analyze data, users can make the best of the conversational power of Google Home or Amazon Echo.

2. Because of the instability of people who rent a room, it makes the device more portable when people are connecting the Internet.

PROTOTYPE ( Form Exploration)

In a nod to change the screen when twisting knob, I made a knob by using 3D print technology. After buying an OLED display, I attempted to present the visual feedback of twisting knob. However, it turned out to be in vain. Later on, another way of prototyping experiences was put forward at that time. Firstly, I made a fake stove paper using foam core and compute required to be covered by 3D printed knob and stove paper. As a result, I decided to open a hole in the surface of a stove cover to let the interface under the knob to show up. From another angle, The artificial stove made by foam core can draw people’s attention to the scenario and bring about a kind of fantastic experience.

KEY LEARNING

1. It is more beneficial to use foam core to build a low-fidelity scenario. This kind of procedure makes it easier for users to arouse empathy.

2. Generally speaking, the system of IoT is more complicated than a single application implemented on a smartphone because the IoT system should be correspondent to reality, actuator, sensor, etc. Besides, different kinds of challenges are on the way such as connecting wifi will use much power, the power challenge for IoT devices.

3. Speaking of IoT product, its network plays a vital role in the stage of design of UI and UX.

4. As an essential part of the product, off-line states is responsible for the whole process. To some extent, since IoT device often connects intermittently. t, there is a potential and high risk of latency as well as unsteady network. Conversely, the majority of people prefer to rapid response. Therefore, nothing can be of greater importance than the exit plan.

WHAT WENT WELL

1. When testing, people expressed more excitements than expected about the product, which is a promising sign.

2. the whole process of prototype and test are proceeding very well. As for users, it is not only a practical device but also a fantastic experience.
