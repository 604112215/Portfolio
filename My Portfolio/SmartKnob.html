SMART KNOB

Have you ever been annoyed by over-cooking food and struggled multi-task by hands when cooking food?

Smart Knob is an IoT device that transfers your old stove to a smart one by taking the place of the old knob. The Smart Knob is embedded in a voice control system like Amazon Echo, Google home. It is conducive to your cooking experience such as taking control of heat by communicating with Echo.

Smart Knob can relieve your burden of hands by communicating with Echo or other sound control devices. It can assist you in adjusting heat temperature.

4. Project Recipe to Smart Knob

Users could also download a recipe from supporting app and project it on the Smart Know. The responsibility of adjusting heat temperature in different steps when cooking will hand to the Smart Kobe. Users can stop the cooking programming or change heating temperature by twisting the Smart Knob at any situations (disconnected status). It let user can fully control the device.

INTERVIEW

BEGINNING

This project aimed at designing an IoT related project. In fact, since a wide array of innovative designers has obtained plenty of accomplishments in this field, there is little chance to continue to carry on IoT related project.

I determined to interview several friends and asked: “ What device do you think are still stupid in your home?”

I noticed that one of my interviewees has the desire of getting a smarter stove.

INITIAL IDEAS

I came up with some initial ideas/sketches while doing an interview. It led me to the current project.

VALUE PROPOSITION STATEMENT

To better define the design scope, I made a value proposition statement to narrow down.

PROBLEMS SCENARIO

To delve deeply into practical situations while cooking, I put forward a Problems Scenario and highlighted some pain points users might have.

PAIN POINT

1. Unit Conversion
The temperature showed in recipes cannot map to the stove perfectly.

2.Rental Apartment
An increasing number of young people choose to rent rooms, especially in a metropolis. It is impossible for them to change the whole stove for a rental apartment. Users want small and portable device.

3.Chaos comes from multi-tasks
People used to handle a lot of tasks at the same time while cooking, such as preparing the rest of ingredients, adjusting heat temperature, keeping eyes on timer, ect. To be honest, completing all those tasks at the same time is not easy. That is where chaos come from.

INSIGHT​S

1.Conversational Interface​​

Users can set heating temperature by talk to the Smart Knob and set a temperature for x minutes.

2.Coordinate heat change with recipes

A user can follow a recipe without controlling heating temperature. The heating temperature will automatically change following recipe.

SITE MAP

Network of IoT

The network that behind the product is crucial to UX design. The system of IoT I choose will exert significant influence on how the User Experience looks like. Instead of using the gateway as a bridge to connect a device with internet. I tend to use WIFI Router as a bridge to embed Smart Knob to network. Here are two significant advantages of this choice:

1. Using Wifi as a gateway enable the Smart Knob to communicate with Google Home or Amazon Echo efficiently.

2. Because of the instability of living situation, this choice makes the device more portable.

PROTOTYPE ( Form Exploration)

I made a physical knob by using 3D print technology. I attempted to present the visual feedback of twisting knob by using OLED display. However, it turned out to be in vain. Another way of prototyping experiences was: First, made a fake stove using foam core and combine it with a 3D printed knob by opening a hole on the surface of the fake stove and put the interface of knob under the fake stove. This rapid prototype- fake stove- can draw people’s attention to the scenario and present this fantastic experience.

KEY LEARNING

1. It is more beneficial to use foam core to build a low-fidelity scenario. This kind of procedure makes it easier for users to arouse empathy.

2. Generally speaking, the system of IoT is more complicated than a single application implemented on a smartphone because the IoT system should be correspondent to reality, actuator, sensor, etc. Besides, different kinds of challenges are on the way such as connecting wifi will use much power, the power challenge for IoT devices.

3. Speaking of IoT product, its network plays a vital role in the stage of design of UI and UX.

4. As an essential part of the product, off-line states is responsible for the whole process. To some extent, since IoT device often connects intermittently. t, there is a potential and high risk of latency as well as unsteady network. Conversely, the majority of people prefer to rapid response. Therefore, nothing can be of greater importance than the exit plan.

WHAT WENT WELL

1. When testing, people expressed more excitements than expected about the product, which is a promising sign.

2. the whole process of prototype and test are proceeding very well. As for users, it is not only a practical device but also a fantastic experience.
